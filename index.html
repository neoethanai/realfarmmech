About Farmmech 108

FarmMech108 is a comprehensive agribusiness consultancy and service provider based in Gandhinagar, Gujarat, dedicated to empowering the agricultural and allied sectors across India. Specializing in farm machinery testing, subsidy empanelment, and EOI/tender/bidding support, FarmMech108 helps clients navigate complex government and institutional procedures.

The company offers end-to-end solutions in franchisee development for farm machinery and dairy products, business and channel development, import-export facilitation, and farm machinery trading. It also provides strategic support for product development and launch, project management, and digital marketing to help businesses grow sustainably.

In addition, FarmMech108 assists with GEM portal registration, product listing, bidding, and event management, while offering guidance on obtaining ISO and other certifications. With a strong focus on enabling clients through technology and innovation, it also offers CRM solutions tailored to agribusiness needs.
